# Shutter Mechanism - SolidWorks Design

An engineering design and assembly project of a mechanical shutter mechanism, developed as part of the Engineering Drawing coursework at Namal University Mianwali.

## 🔧 Project Overview
* **Project Title:** Shutter Mechanism
* **Software Used:** SolidWorks
* **Project Type:** Team Project

This project involves the individual modeling and geometric dimensioning of mechanical parts, followed by a constrained assembly to replicate smooth opening and closing mechanical motions.


## 🛠️ Components List
The complete mechanism consists of the following precision-modeled parts:
* **Base Plate:** The structural foundation holding the pins and ring.
* **Center Ring:** Actuates the motion across all blades simultaneously.
* **Shutter Blades:** Overlapping plates that create the iris opening/closing effect.
* **Pins:** Mechanical pivots linking the blades, ring, and base plate.


## 🚀 Design Methodology & Features
Each component was engineered using industry-standard CAD practices:
1. **Parametric Sketching:** Fully defined sketches with precise dimensioning.
2. **Features:** Applied extrusions, cut features, fillets, and circular patterns.
3. **Assembly Mates:** Utilized concentric, coincident, and advanced mechanical mates to achieve realistic mechanical motion.



## 🎯 Key Learning Outcomes
* Advanced part modeling and geometric dimensioning in SolidWorks.
* Implementing assembly constraints and mechanical mates.
* Kinematic analysis of basic iris and shutter mechanisms.
* The critical role of geometric tolerances in CAD design.
* Collaborative engineering workflow and task delegation.
